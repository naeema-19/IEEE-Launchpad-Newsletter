About

Launchpad

One of the most prestigious event of IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn that is determined to serve the goal of creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates. Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to create meaningful connections that drive progress and innovation in Kerala's job market.

Call to Action

Are you a student seeking Networking Opportunities, Access to Industry Insights, Exposure to Diverse Career Paths, and Personal Development?

You'll gain all these perks and more by participating in LaunchPad.

So, what are you waiting for? Registration is still open and candidates can apply by following the registration process outlined below.



Register Now

  • Step 1: Online Registration Form

    Candidates interested in participating in the job fair should register or log in to the Digital Workforce Management Portal. Once logged in, apply for Job-29580 LaunchPad Kerala 2024-Job Fair, providing the necessary details.

  • Step 2: Online Registration on LaunchPad Website

    After successfully applying to LaunchPad Kerala 2024 through the Digital Workforce Management Portal, candidates must submit additional details via the registration form on the LaunchPad website.

  • Step 3: Confirmation

    Candidates will receive an email from the LaunchPad Kerala 2024 team after registration closes. The next steps will be communicated either through email or text messages. All updates will also be posted on the updates page of the LaunchPad Kerala 2024 website.
